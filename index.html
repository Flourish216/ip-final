Post-War Dimensional Access Program

After the Collapse, a skeletal department kept the surviving slimdoors alive. We catalog what remains, weld the unstable hinges, and log every resonance frequency by hand.

Field Archivist / Survivor: You walk the aisles, note the anomalies, and listen for the hum right before a door shivers.

Maintenance Crew: Rotating engineers keep the rails cold, the LEDs honest, and the vault sealed between scans.

Protocol: Every entry demands a scan, a story, and a signature. Unstable gates are sealed unless Command signs off.

Only the family activation cube can unlock archived doors.
